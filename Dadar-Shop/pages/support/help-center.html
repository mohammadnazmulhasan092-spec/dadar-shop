Orders

How do I place an order?

Browse through our catalog, select products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Choose your delivery address and payment method to complete your order.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

If your order hasn't been processed yet, you can modify or cancel it from your account. Contact our support team for assistance.

Is there a minimum order value?

Most items have no minimum order value. However, some sellers may have minimum purchase requirements displayed on their product pages.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for large orders. Please contact our B2B team at business@dadarshop.com for more information.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 day delivery. Delivery times vary by location.

What are the shipping charges?

Free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping is $5, express shipping is $10. Some items qualify for free shipping regardless of order value.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your order confirmation email. Visit our Track Order page for real-time updates.

Do you deliver internationally?

We currently deliver to 8 countries in South and Southeast Asia. Check our shipping policy page for a complete list of delivery destinations.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile wallets (bKash, Nagad), bank transfers, and cash on delivery (COD).

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to protect your payment information. Your data is safe with us.

Can I save my payment method?

Yes, you can save multiple payment methods in your account for faster checkout on future purchases.

Do you offer installment plans?

Yes, select credit cards offer installment payment options for eligible purchases over $200. Check at checkout for available options.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging with all accessories included.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to My Orders, select the item to return, and submit a return request. Follow the instructions to arrange a pickup.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and verify the returned item.

What about damaged items?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us immediately with photos. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund.
