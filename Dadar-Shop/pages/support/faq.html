Frequently Asked Questions

Click on any question to expand and view the answer.

Account & Registration

How do I create a Dadar Shop account? ▼
Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email address and create a password, then verify your email. You can also sign up using your social media accounts for faster registration.
Is it free to create an account? ▼
Yes, creating a Dadar Shop account is completely free. You only pay for items you purchase and applicable shipping charges.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password.
How do I update my profile information? ▼
Go to your Account Settings and click "Edit Profile." You can update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture. Changes take effect immediately.

Shopping & Orders

Can I change my order after placing it? ▼
Orders can be modified only before they're dispatched. Go to your order page and click "Modify Order." If your order has already been shipped, you'll need to return it or contact our support team.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Visa, Mastercard, debit cards, mobile wallets (bKash, Nagad), bank transfers, and cash on delivery (COD). All payment methods are secure and encrypted.
Are there any hidden charges? ▼
No, all charges are displayed clearly before you complete your purchase. The final price includes product cost, shipping, and any applicable taxes. No hidden charges are added.
Do you offer international shipping? ▼
Yes, we ship to 8 countries across South and Southeast Asia. Check our Shipping Policy page for the complete list of countries where we deliver.

Products & Categories

How do I search for specific products? ▼
Use the search bar at the top of the page to enter your product name. You can also filter by category, brand, price range, and customer ratings to narrow down your search.
Are product prices fixed? ▼
Prices can vary based on seller, demand, and promotions. We recommend comparing prices and checking for deals. Prices are locked once you add items to your cart.
How do I know if a product is genuine? ▼
All sellers on Dadar Shop are verified. We have a strict policy against counterfeit products. Check seller ratings and product reviews. Report any suspicious items immediately.
Can I view similar products? ▼
Yes, on each product page you'll find a "Similar Products" section showing related items. You can also use our recommendations based on your browsing history.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery usually take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 day delivery to selected areas. Delivery times may vary based on location and product availability.
What are the shipping costs? ▼
Free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping costs $5, express shipping costs $10. Some items and sellers offer free shipping regardless of order value.
How do I track my order? ▼
You'll receive a tracking number via email. Visit our Track Order page, enter your tracking number, and get real-time updates on your package location and delivery status.
What if my package arrives damaged? ▼
Contact our support team immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a full refund or replacement at no cost to you within 48 hours of delivery.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? ▼
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Products must be unused and in original packaging with all accessories included. Some items like food and cosmetics cannot be returned.
How do I initiate a return? ▼
Log into your account, go to My Orders, select the item, and click "Return Item." Fill out the return reason and follow the instructions. A return label will be generated for you to arrange a pickup.
How long does a refund take? ▼
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.
Will I be charged for return shipping? ▼
If the item is defective or incorrect, return shipping is free. For other returns, the return shipping cost will be deducted from your refund amount.

Didn't find your answer?

We're here to help! Contact our support team or visit our help center for more assistance.
