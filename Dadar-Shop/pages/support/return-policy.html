Return Policy

At Dadar Shop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we'll help you return or exchange your item.

Return Conditions

Items must meet the following conditions for return:

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • All tags and labels must be attached
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods (groceries, food items)
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Opened cosmetics and personal care items
  • Digital products and gift cards

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your Dadar Shop account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and select the order
  3. Click "Request Return" and select the item(s)
  4. Choose return reason and upload photos if damaged
  5. Wait for approval (usually within 24-48 hours)
  6. Pack the item securely with original packaging
  7. Schedule a pickup or drop at the nearest center

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method.
