Our Journey

2015 - Founded

Dadar Shop was founded with a vision to revolutionize online shopping in South Asia. Starting with just 50 sellers, we quickly gained customer trust through quality products and reliable service.

2017 - Rapid Growth

Expanded operations to multiple cities and introduced mobile app. User base grew to 500K and we achieved break-even profitability.

2019 - Market Leader

Became the leading online marketplace in the region with 2M users and 10K sellers. Introduced flash sales and expanded product categories.

2024 - Global Expansion

Reached 5M users with presence in 8 countries across South Asia and Southeast Asia. Became industry benchmark for customer satisfaction.
